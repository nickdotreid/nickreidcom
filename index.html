    I worked with epidemiologists to experiment with HIV epidemiology data while working at the HIV Prevention Section of the San Francisco Department of Public Health. I designed and developed a social chart analysis and annotation tool, which aimed to create create a real time social environment for data. I presented this project at APHA in 2012.
